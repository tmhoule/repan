Application Views

The main screens in the app — click to select the ones you'd like to discuss further

1

Login Screen

Simple name picker — avatars with names, click yours to enter. No passwords. Manager can access account management from here.

2

My Tasks (Staff Home)

Your personal task list sorted by urgency score. Shows status, priority badge, due date, percent complete bar, and streak/points summary at the top. Quick actions to update progress, add comments, or mark done. Celebration animation on completion.

3

Task Detail / Edit

Full task view — all fields editable. Activity log showing every update, comment, status change with timestamps. Progress slider. Blocker flag toggle. Comment box. Shows who created it, who it's assigned to, and full history.

4

Backlog

Unassigned tasks ordered by priority. Staff can browse and claim items. Shows effort estimate, priority, and the forecasted "estimated start" timeline. Drag to reorder (manager). Backlog size counter and total effort summary at top.

5

Team View

See all team members, their current tasks, streaks, and recent badges. Not a leaderboard — just visibility into what everyone's working on. Click a person to see their full task list.

6

Manager Dashboard

The command center. Sections include:

  • Workload distribution — tasks per person, who's overloaded vs underloaded
  • At-risk tasks — overdue, stalled, or blocked items surfaced prominently
  • Backlog health — total size, effort breakdown, estimated weeks of work
  • Weekly throughput — tasks and effort points completed, trending up or down
  • Team activity — recent updates across all tasks
7

Reports

Weekly/monthly summaries. What got done, what slipped, backlog trend over time, team throughput chart, individual contribution summaries. Exportable or printable.

8

Profile & Achievements

Your badges, points history, streaks, and stats. Visible to the whole team. Shows total tasks completed, on-time rate, current streaks, and all earned badges with dates.

9

Admin (Manager Only)

Create/edit user accounts, manage badge definitions, create new badges, system settings. Accessible only to manager role.
